# Anxiety

Common in MCI and mild AD; often a predictor of progression rather than a late-stage feature.

## Sensor signature

- **EDA:** elevated (sympathetic arousal)
- **HR:** elevated, often with reduced HRV
- **Activity:** normal or *decreased* (key contrast vs. agitation, which has activity↑)

## Distinguishing from agitation

Anxiety = arousal *without* externalizing motor activation; agitation = arousal *with* it. Same EDA/HR pattern, different IMU profile.

## Intervention

Reassurance, environmental stability, light exercise, mindfulness-style audio prompts. See [Intervention agent](../agents/intervention_agent.md).
